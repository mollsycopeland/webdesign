La La Land Synopsis:

"La La Land" is a romantic musical film that follows the love story between a struggling jazz musician (Sebastian, photographed on the right) and an aspiring actress (Mia, photograghed on the left) in Los Angeles. As they pursue their dreams, their relationship faces challenges, testing their commitment to each other and their individual aspirations. The film is a vibrant and bittersweet exploration of love, ambition, and the pursuit of happiness in the City of Angels.
